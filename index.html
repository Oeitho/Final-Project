There are plenty of bike theft-prevention products available on the market. Yet one third of norwegians have experienced a bike theft, so do these products still hold up? We wanted people to start using their bikes, everywhere, without the fear of it being stolen. Therefore, we designed the solution and came up with productname, a simple way to keep your bike safe.

Unit

Productname consists of a sensor-acitivated GPS unit with an inbuilt transceiver, all hidden inside your bike's seatpost. This means that no matter what happens to your bike, you'll know where it is at all times.

App

It comes with a unique serial number used to create a joint account for the app and website. You'll get a notification on your phone if anything were to happen, you can then track your bike in real time via the app or website.

keyring

A simple pre-synced keyring is part of the package as an alternative to the app. It lights up in one of the three different colors if anything happens to your bike and can be turned off by simple pressing the center button.
